DemoFour

Paste your website. Record your voice. Get a polished product demo video in under 60 seconds.

<60s to generate
1 click workflow
6 AI components
01

Motivation

Founders waste hours — sometimes days — trying to produce a single demo video. They juggle screen recorders, freelancers, and half-learned video editors, all while racing toward a launch deadline. The process is slow, expensive, and unpredictable. Most early-stage teams skip demo content entirely or settle for low-quality screen captures that fail to convey the polish of their product.

02

User & Business Value

DemoFour targets early-stage founders, indie developers, and product marketers who need professional demo content without a production budget. A compelling demo video can be the difference between a click and a bounce on a landing page, between funding and a pass in a pitch deck. By reducing creation time to under a minute, DemoFour turns demo production from a blocker into a habit — something teams do on every release, not just once a year.

03

Components to Build

The system comprises several coordinated pieces: a React UI for URL input and voice recording, a web scraper + RAG pipeline to extract and understand website content, the Claude API for generating narration scripts from that content, the ElevenLabs API for voice cloning from a 10-second user clip, an agentic orchestration layer (PydanticAI) to coordinate the full pipeline, and pre-built video templates for final assembly.
